SLUSH'D

Slush’D events are independent entrepreneurship events led by local ecosystem players with the goal to foster local entrepreneurship agendas while tapping into the Slush global community.

NYUAD Slush’D is led by Violet Ventures at NYUAD, selected as one of the 6 Slush’D partnership teams for 2022 and was the only university-level partner amongst the cohort.

The specific bottlenecks NYUAD Slush’D aims to tackle are threefold: 1) many youth do not yet see entrepreneurship as a way to contribute social good to society 2) despite having disruptive and impact-driven students, talent is still largely driven to major corporations 3) our students lack the first steps on the brainstorming process behind venture building, especially for international students in this region.

THE HULT PRIZE

The Hult Prize challenges young people around the world to solve the planet's most pressing issues through social entrepreneurship. Every year, a prompt is released and the team with the best pitch will receive 1 million dollars to execute their social enterprise, for real.

This year's prompt is redesigning fashion - an attempt to better a world that is drowning with climate change and labor supply chain problems.

Violet Ventures has paired with the Hult Prize Foundation to select NYUAD’s representative for the regional Hult Prize summit in Dubai, March 2023. The winning team will go to Boston to compete for the grand prize.

Our 2 - day NYUAD Slush’D event will serve as a preparatory bootcamp for the Hult Global Finals and aims to equip 12 teams with the tools and skills needed forthe brainstorming and ideation process.

3 weeks later, on November 26th, Violet Ventures will host the Hult Prize Competition Pitch, where NYUAD’s Hult Prize representative will be ultimately chosen. The selected team will go through further rounds of training and mentorship prior to the summit which will take place in March 2023.

Ayoola Brimmo

Ayoola is an Engineer and social entrepreneur. He co-founded Cycled Technologies in 2016 to deliver waste management products and services that help buildings and municipalities recycle more and reduce logistics expenses. Since then, he has led operations at the company and championed the development of the Cycled Self Sorting SmartBin Technology which earned him an “MIT Technology Reviews Innovator Under 35” recognition. Ayoola also holds a PhD in Mechanical Engineering and by virtue of his scientific exploits, he was involved in the development of the Non-contact Multiphysics Probe, which is a groundbreaking addition to the toolkit of the Human Atlas Project -- the world's most ambitious genetic research project. Prior to co-founding Cycled Technologies, Ayoola served as the Executive Director of Nordoest which was the Middle East’s Representative of Innovation Norway — the Norwegian Government’s most important instrument for innovation and development of Norwegian enterprises. He also served as the Technical Director of Nordic Innovation Hub — a cleantech incubator in Masdar City Abu Dhabi, set up as a collaboration between the five Nordic countries and supported by Nordic Innovation. Ayoola will be speaking in the founder panel about passion and mission building through his venture.

Joel Alexander

Joel Alexander is the Co-Founder & CEO of CrowdPad, formerly working where they're building the next wave of social media by ensuring that creators are able to monetize since day 1, immune to deplatforming and demonetization plays by big-tech platforms. CrowdPad recently raised $2.5M backed by Balaji S, IVC, Ali Abdaal, FTX Ventures (Serum Ecosystem Fund) & others, was featured as one of Sifted's 13 European Web3 Startups to Watch, and was also named a top creator economy company by both Antler & SpeedInvest (not investors in CrowdPad). His background includes working for several top European startups in the creator economy & edtech categories, he is also a bit of a YouTuber himself having hustled his way into VC & Tech by doing podcasts with YC-backed founders. Joel will be speaking in the founder panel about passion and mission building through his venture.

Dr. Barbara Scheck

Barbara Scheck is Professor of Entrepreneurship at Munich Business School / Germany and Visiting Faculty at NYUAD. She is the Co-Founder of several startups in the impact space and focuses on the financing of social enterprises, impact and mission-related investing, and impact assessment in her research. Other significant experiences include consulting for a social venture capital fund and projects for development cooperation agencies on social entrepreneurship.This together with the time she has spent on startup workshopping, leads her to have tangible awareness on startup roadmapping and key building blocks. Barbara will be leading workshops in NYUAD Slush’D.

Simran Chowdhry

Simran is the Co-founder of BluePhin Technologies, Co-founder of Airtap, a startup Advisor, a UNGC Youth Ambassador and Finance graduate from the American University of Sharjah. She is a passionate, self-taught techie and social entrepreneur whose mission is to save the planet by decarbonizing our economy and pave the way for green innovation. Simran has been featured on Entrepreneur ME, CNN, Dubai Eye and several other sustainability podcasts for her unique journey building innovative solutions for environmental conservation. After doing several startup weekends and continuously taking part in every competition that came her way, she has since won the Sharjah EF Pitch Competition, Unilever's Young Entrepreneur Award, exhibited at GMIS, Gitex Future Stars and others. Simran will be leading several workshops in Slush’D x Violet Ventures and will be speaking about her experiences as a young founder in the UAE.

Dr. Bhaskar Dasgupta

Dr. Bhaskar Dasgupta has extensive knowledge in the entrepreneurship and investment ecosystems in EMEA, having served as the Head of Market Infrastructure, Digital and VC/FinTech for South Asia at ADGM, and as strategic advisors for various Fintech initiatives, amongst many other roles. At ADGM, he was responsible for developing the UAE ecosystem, which saw a substantial increase in Digital Banking, Venture Capital, FinTech and Sustainable Finance companies listed in ADGM. Amongst the companies he serves as a Strategic Advisor for include Peregrine Digital (decentralized investment platform), RuDo Wealth (personal finance management), Masary Capital (MENA’s first Emirati owned blockchain and crypto joint venture platform), Saffe (facial recognition technology) and Sheikh Hamdan Bin Ahmad Al Makhtoum’s Private Office. Dr. Bhaskar Dasgupta will be leading the session on the MENA ecosystem together with Devika Raveendran and will be speaking about the UAE economy, common sectors, key customers, fundraising, and startup environments.

Devika Raveendran

Devika Raveendran is the Founder and CEO of DR Partners- a boutique corporate and wealth structuring advisory practice headquartered in Dubai with presence in Abu Dhabi and Delhi. As a Private Clients lawyer with diverse experience working for global trust companies and multifamily offices, she has been actively advising family enterprises in structuring their personal and business wealth using multi-generational & jurisdictional legacy structures, engaging with members for implementing family governance, coaching the rising generation for leadership & business succession along with crafting residency & relocation plans for ultra-high net worth families. Devika is a cross border structuring expert advising families and corporates on consolidation of complex business ownership and restructuring for externalization of ownership to create tax optimized businesses. As a strategic advisor, she has been leveraging her 15+ years of experience in legal and corporate advisory to help early-stage companies & founders optimize their market entry in the region & facilitate access to capital from her well-established network of investors. Amongst the several entities where she serves as an Advisory board Member, Sterve is the first ever AI powered integrated platform in the Middle East & Africa region for MSMEs to manage their commerce and finance through one app. The other notable startup is Mirathi that uses innovative technology for safekeeping of cryptos and seamless transfer of digital legacy. Devika will be hosting the session on the MENA ecosystem together with Dr Bhaskar Dasgupta and will be speaking about an overview on key legal considerations for business owners in UAE, ranging from licensing to residency.

Aman Merchant

A mission-driven serial parallel entrepreneur, futurist, investor & advisor with 25+ years experience across 12 ventures in education, social impact and disruptive innovation, Aman is the Chief Provocateur at Radicle, passionate about transforming industries, cities and lives through ideating and launching ambitious platforms for solving the wicked system problems that challenge humanity today. While co-curating RewirEd (global education summit) for Dubai Cares, he launched the MENAP region’s first Edtech collective (ReimaginED Collective), having previously managed a Global Grand Challenges program for Expo2020 & a disruptive innovation program for Dubai Airport Free Zone, curated Abundance 360 Dubai (CEO Summit with Singularity University) with Dr Peter Diamandis, launched Epiq360 (regional e-commerce & coaching platform for wellness/longevity/peak performance) and co-founded & led Impact Hub Dubai (global entrepreneurial network for positive impact). A member of both the Young Presidents Organisation (YPO) & Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO), he holds a 1st Class Masters (Hons) Degree from Oxford University & is also a Stanford Business School & London School of Economics alum. An Entrepreneur Mentor in Residence at London Business School since 2014, he is the MENA region’s 1st Impact Investing Fellow at AVPN, UAE’s 1st Fellow with OnDeck Edtech as well as a certified Peak Performance coach from the Flow Genome Project. He serves on the Global Council on SDG4, and was part of the investment committee at a $100m global impact fund (ExpoLive). Flow, biohacking, venture creation, system thinking, moonshots, global education systems & edtech are some of his research and teaching interests which he is pursuing at NYUAD. At Slush’D, Aman will be hosting workshops and sharing insights stemming from his background and passion for societal and corporate problem solving, innovation, and technology.
